Welcome to our sample web application! The Cat Shop is for demonstration purposes only.

Overview

This is a functional web app that is PCI compliant. It is an e-commerce application selling daily supplies for cat thus hypothetical customers should be able to browse products by category, filter them, then add them to their cart to be purchased. If they choose to checkout, they have the option to log in, or continue as guests and fill out their information. After filling out the shipping information and going through a credit card transaction, they receive confirmation of purchase.

Objective

The overarching goal of our project is to enable customers to reduce the burden of certifying their systems built on Oracle Cloud Infrastructure (OCI), be more secure, and move to OCI more quickly and with regulated workloads.
